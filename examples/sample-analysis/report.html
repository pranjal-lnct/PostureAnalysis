Recommended Corrective Exercises

Personalized Based on Your Analysis

Important Notice

These exercises are AI-generated recommendations. Please consult with a licensed physiotherapist before starting any exercise program, especially if you experience pain or have existing medical conditions.

1

Chin Tucks

Gently retract chin backward (like making a double chin), hold for 5 seconds. Keep eyes level.

Frequency: 3 sets of 10 reps, 2x daily

Strengthens deep neck flexors and reduces forward head posture

2

Thoracic Extensions

Place hands behind head, gently extend upper back over a foam roller or rolled towel. Hold 30 seconds.

Frequency: 3-5 repetitions, 1-2x daily

Improves thoracic spine mobility and reduces excessive kyphosis

3

Scapular Retractions

Squeeze shoulder blades together as if holding a pencil between them. Hold for 5 seconds.

Frequency: 3 sets of 15 reps, 2x daily

Strengthens rhomboids and middle trapezius to improve shoulder posture

4

Pelvic Tilts

Lie on back with knees bent. Flatten lower back against floor by tilting pelvis. Hold 5 seconds.

Frequency: 3 sets of 12 reps, 1-2x daily

Activates core muscles and normalizes lumbar curve

5

Quadriceps Strengthening

Seated leg extensions with slight knee bend. Focus on controlled movement without locking knees.

Frequency: 3 sets of 10 reps, 3x weekly

Improves knee control and reduces hyperextension tendency

6

Postural Awareness Practice

Stand against wall with heels, buttocks, shoulders, and head touching. Hold 30 seconds while breathing normally.

Frequency: 2-3 times daily

Develops kinesthetic awareness of optimal alignment

Pro Tip: Start slowly and focus on proper form. Consistency is more important than intensity. Take photos every 2-3 weeks to track your progress.
